My Journey

Rishabh Keshan | 24/04/2020

My name is Rishabh Keshan and today, I am here to share my fitness journey. Hope this motivates you to start working on your health.

Staying in Kolkata, I have always been fond of sweets, puchkas, kachori, samosas and other delicious street foods. Always been called names like 'Doraemon', 'Haathi', 'Motu' never affected me.

I had just completed my boards when the above picture was clicked.Then came my JEE days in which I used to just eat, sleep and study. There were no outdoor activities, just going to school and coming back. In two years, I had gained more than 20kgs and I weighed 104kgs; my highest. After JEE ended, I got admitted to Vellore Institute of Technology, Vellore as a Computer Science student.
(P.S. - Why I couldn't go to IIT is for some other day.)


I had realized that I had to do something about my health. I used to feel weak, would get tired after walking a few metres.I started jogging every morning, practiced Intermittent Fasting and stopped eating junk food and this was the result after 8 months :

I had lost 42kgs in a span of 8 months. I was feeling more energetic, I could run for miles and I had never felt so good before. Friends, family and relatives were all in praises. I was satisfied with whatever I had done. I know I have a long way to go. Healthy eating has become more like a lifestyle than a diet. The best advice I would like to give anyone is that Never Belittle Yourself. Have patience and keep working on yourself till you achieve your goals. Have patience and trust yourself. Make Healthy Living a lifestyle. Will share more tips in some other blog. Till then, take care.


All progress takes place outside the comfort zone.
